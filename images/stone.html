The Roadside Stone

I was lying there

in a roadside half dead.

I was injured,

I had no food, no bed

One Day I was picked

And brought to hell

I was drilled and reshaped

I yelled but accepted a farewell

Now I am head of heads

God of people

My operation was successful

A roadside stone now owns a temple

Being stepping stones

Marble and golds are jealous of me

They don't know my sacrifices but you and me!
