TREE

We all are the branches of the same tree

All free and ready to grow

Mother grabs the soil to protect us all

but don't know the direction of the storm blow.

We breath same air, drink same water

We get equal love of a mother

we bloom on same mother's heart,

a fruit and a flower.

We grow old together

a lot of fun

creaking in the wind and

dance like a pendulum.

Mother feeds us as our need

let's not demand for more

no more fight each other

let love come, open the door.

Let be stronger against storm

and raise our voice against those selfish creature

Let us fight together.

Let us die together.
